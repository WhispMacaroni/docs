<h2>Getting to the Dashboard</h2>
<Steps>
  <Step>
    First, navigate to our dashboard page at https://whisp.bot/dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    If prompted, log in to your Discord account and authorize Whisp to view your
    servers.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Finally, select the server which you wish to configure. This will bring you
    to where you can make changes to your server.
  </Step>
</Steps>
<p>
  Once you are on the dashbard, you can follow any of our other guides to help
  you set up your server.
</p>
