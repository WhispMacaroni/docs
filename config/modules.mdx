<p>
  When you visit the modules section of the dashboard, you will have the option
  to enable and disable any of the modules, which will allow you to use it.
</p>
<Frame>![enabled module example](/images/module.png)</Frame>
<p>
  When you enable a module, it will display on your sidebar and then you will be
  able to configure it.
</p>
